# ECHOME Voice Cloning Script

**Instructions for Recording:**
1. Find a perfectly quiet room with no echo (closets full of clothes work great).
2. Use a high-quality microphone if possible, keeping it about 6-8 inches from your mouth.
3. Read at a natural, conversational pace. Do not speed-read.
4. Express the emotions implied by the punctuation (pause at commas, raise pitch at question marks, show excitement at exclamation points).
5. Ensure your pronunciation of technical terms is exactly how you want your AI clone to say them.
6. Export the final file as a clean, uncompressed `.wav` file.
